# Product Marketing Context: Sonea Labs

## 1. Page Purpose
- **Type:** Agency Landing Page (Direct Response)
- **Primary Action:** Book a scoping call (to get a fixed-price scope and delivery date).

## 2. Audience
- **Ideal Customer:** Solofounders or small venture-backed teams with a clear product idea but lacking technical speed.
- **Problem:** "Development Hell" and "Launch Paralysis." Burned by slow agencies, high costs, or technical overwhelm.
- **Hesitations:** "Will they actually ship?", "Is it going to be a buggy mess?", "How much will this *really* cost?"
- **Language:** "Need to launch," "MVP," "Time to market," "Fixed price," "No fluff."

## 3. Product/Offer
- **Sonea Labs:** A tight engineering team that ships products people actually use.
- **Unique Selling Point (USP):** The 28-day MVP guarantee. "Ship in 28 days or it's free."
- **Key Transformation:** From "Idea/Figma" to "Live Product with First Paying User."
- **Proof Points:** Tijara (GCC Trade SaaS), QDash (Mumbai Nightlife), RespawnIRL (Gaming Recovery), Zoe AI (Therapy AI).

## 4. Brand Voice & Tone
- **Persona:** The No-Bulls**t Senior Engineer.
- **Tone:** Direct, opinionated, high-agency, minimalist, confident.
- **Style:** Punchy, short sentences, active verbs. Avoid corporate jargon ("solutions," "optimize," "leverage").
